Data Deletion Instructions

Your privacy is important to us. If you wish to delete your data associated with MovesApp, please follow the steps below:

  1. Email us at support@movesapp.io with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  2. In the email, include the following details:
    • Your account username or registered email.
    • Reason for deletion (optional).
  3. You will receive a confirmation email once your request has been processed.
